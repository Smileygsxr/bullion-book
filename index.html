Is Bullion Book really free?

Yes - Bullion Book is completely free. Create an account to sync your journal to your private login, or try it without an account and your data stays in your own browser.

Can I import trades from my broker?

Yes. You can import your broker's CSV trade history (for example a MetaTrader 5 export) and Bullion Book keeps the broker's own reported P&L. You can also log trades manually with entries, exits, fees, stop-loss and target levels.

Is my trading data private?

Yes. Your journal lives under your own private login and is never shown to anyone else unless you explicitly share a snapshot. There is also a one-click privacy blur for streaming or trading in public places.

Which instruments does Bullion Book support?

You can journal any symbol - gold, forex, indices, crypto or stocks. Chart-based extras like MAE/MFE and volatility analysis use bundled daily candle data for XAUUSD, BTCUSD, US500 and the major forex pairs.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Bullion Book runs entirely in your web browser on desktop and mobile - nothing to download or install.
